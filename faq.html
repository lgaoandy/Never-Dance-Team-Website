Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some helpful FAQs!

Our practice time is every Friday 3-6PM, but can go to 8PM at the lastest.


We mainly practice at the YMCA in Eau Claire, or the Central Library.


In the summer, we may have more practice times in the week depending on member availability.


Covers are broken down to 4 practices days and 1 filming day.
During practice, we primarily focus on positions and getting everyone in sync. Throughout the project, the group will decide on filming locations and outfits.


Once selected into a cover, you are expected to learn sections of the dance in your own time ahead of practice.


Song selection is predominantly based upon what our members want to cover. When the amount of members interested matches the number requirement, it can become a cover.


Every year during summer, we will compete in the SSK korean competition in Calgary. There also has been organizations who contacted us for live performance opportunities.


You will be given the opportunity to join us on a cover for a trial period. During this time, our manager will assess your commitment, skills and how you interact with the rest of the group. Once you pass the assessment, you will be officially accepted as a member and you will be added to our Facebook group page where all important information about cover projects and practices will be, as well as our facebook messenger group chat.
